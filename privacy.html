Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Snackable Games ("we", "us") provides web games at snackable-games.com. We respect your privacy and aim to be transparent about what data we collect and why.

Data we collect

  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 to measure usage (page views, game events). GA4 collects device information and anonymized IP data.
  • Ads: We use Google AdSense to display ads. AdSense may use cookies to personalize ads and measure performance.
  • Browser Fingerprint: We use anonymous browser characteristics (screen size, timezone, browser type) to prevent duplicate game submissions and spam. This creates a hash that cannot identify you personally and is not used for tracking across sites.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies and third-party cookies (Google) for analytics and advertising. You can control cookies via your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect some functionality.

ads.txt

We publish an ads.txt file to declare authorized ad sellers. /ads.txt

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share aggregated/anonymous analytics with Google. We only disclose data if required by law.

Feedback

We offer an optional feedback form. If you submit feedback, we collect your message and optionally your email (if you provide it for follow-up).

Your rights

Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your data: snackable.apps@gmail.com

Third-party links

Our games may link to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.
