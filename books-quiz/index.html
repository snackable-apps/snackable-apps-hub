📖 How to Play Books Quiz

Books Quiz is a daily literary guessing game. Your goal is to identify the mystery book using clues about its author, genre, and publication details.

📚 The Rules

  1. Guess a book — Type any book title and select from the dropdown.
  2. Read the feedback — Each attribute shows how your guess compares to the secret book.
  3. Deduce the answer — Use logic to narrow down the possibilities.

📊 Available Clues

Each guess reveals information about:

  • Author — Who wrote the book
  • Author Nationality — The author's country of origin
  • Genre — Literary category (Fiction, Mystery, Sci-Fi, etc.)
  • Publication Year — When the book was first published
  • Page Count — Length of the book
  • Original Language — Language the book was written in
💡 Pro tip: The author's nationality and original language are powerful clues. Combine them with the publication era to quickly narrow down your search!
